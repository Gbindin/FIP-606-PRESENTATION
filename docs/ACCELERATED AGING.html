RESULT: There was significant difference (P˂0.001) among the three sowing dates. Likewise, the interaction between variety and sowing date was highly significant (P˂0.001). The highest germination percentage was recorded in TVx 3236, which was statistically similar to SAMPEA 11 but significantly differed from the other varieties.

The normality and homoscedasticity of the dataset analyzed indicated that both normality and homoscedasticity displayed an even distribution. Based on this, the dataset exhibited an evenly distributed normality and homoscedasticity.
